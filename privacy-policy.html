Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how EVE Systems collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information in connection with our AI-assisted communications, lead operations, voice workflows, and messaging services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact and business data: business name, phone number, email, city/state, and operational contact details.
  • Communication data: call metadata, timestamps, dispositions, summaries, transcripts, and message delivery data.
  • System data: device/network logs, API request metadata, security events, and reliability telemetry.
  • User-provided data: details submitted through forms, replies, support requests, or onboarding flows.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve outreach, intake, and follow-up workflows.
  • Measure response quality, routing accuracy, and operational performance.
  • Maintain compliance, security, fraud prevention, and abuse controls.
  • Provide support, troubleshooting, and incident resolution.
  • Send service-related communications and required legal notices.

3. Corporate Practice of Medicine (CPOM) and Clinical Scope

  • EVE Systems provides non-clinical operations, communication, and workflow support only.
  • EVE Systems does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or make medical decisions.
  • Clinical judgment and patient-care decisions remain with licensed professionals and medical leadership.
  • Messages and operational reports are administrative communications and are not medical advice.

4. Legal Basis and Consent

Where required, we process data based on consent, legitimate interests, contract necessity, and legal obligations. For voice and message communications, we apply jurisdiction-specific consent and recording controls, including two-party consent restrictions where applicable.

5. SMS and A2P Messaging Disclosure

By opting in to receive text messages from EVE Systems, you agree to receive service and operational messages. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for support.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers and infrastructure partners (for example voice, telephony, cloud hosting, and analytics providers) solely to deliver and secure services. We do not sell personal information for third-party advertising.

7. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for operational, legal, security, and contractual purposes, then delete or de-identify it where required.

8. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, secret management, audit logs, and monitoring. No system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to opt out of certain communications. You may also request do-not-call or message suppression at any time.

10. Cross-Border Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We use reasonable safeguards where required by law.

11. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

For privacy requests or compliance questions, contact: privacy@eve-systems.ai
